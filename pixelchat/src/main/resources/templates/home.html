How does the image-based

two-factor

authentication work?

PixelChat introduces a unique

authentication system where,

in addition to the

traditional password, users

upload a specific image as an

additional security layer.

Every time you log in, you'll

need to provide this image,

ensuring that even if someone

knows your password, they

can't access your account

without the image.
