Top PixelChat Questions Unraveled
How to customize my chatroom background?

Navigate to 'Chatroom Settings'.

Choose 'Appearance' and pick your preferred theme or upload an image.

Save changes and enjoy your personalized space!

How to report a user?

Click on the user's profile.

Select the 'Report User' option.

Fill in the details and submit. Our team will take action swiftly.

Can I have private chats?

Absolutely! Click on a user's name.

Select 'Start Private Chat' to begin a confidential conversation.

Only you and the user can view this chat.

How to change chatroom name?

Navigate to 'Chatroom Settings'.

Choose 'General' and update the name field.

Save the changes and it's updated instantly!

How to set chatroom admin?

In 'Chatroom Settings', go to 'User Management'.

Click on a user's name and select 'Set as Admin'.

Ensure you trust them with admin privileges!

How to delete a chatroom?

Head to 'Chatroom Settings'.

Scroll to the bottom and select 'Delete Chatroom'.

Confirm your choice and it's done. Use cautiously!

Who can join PixelChat?

Anyone with a valid email can join PixelChat!

We've made it inclusive for everyone to connect and share.

Is the user identity verified?

Yes! We prioritize user safety.

PixelChat has a robust verification process for every user.

Rest easy knowing you're chatting with genuine profiles.

Are chats encrypted?

Absolutely! PixelChat values your privacy.

All chat messages are encrypted end-to-end, ensuring your conversations remain private.
